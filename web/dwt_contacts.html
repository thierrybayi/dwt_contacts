Notes

To display contact's info, just click on the contact's name and let informations appear on the form.

Then you are able to edit informations about this contact and add changes to the list.

When clicking on a contact's link, the contact will disappear from the list(N.B: to get changes just refresh the page. If not, two contacts will appear on the list with the same name) so that on add buttton click, it will be added to list as a new contact.

To delete a contact, just click on its link and push the clear button.

Now enjoy!
